# Claims

- Mechanism claim: humanoid contacts are scarce episode-level commitments across balance, manipulation, and recovery.
- Method claim: budgeting contacts with recovery reserve, manipulation reserve, balance margin, and contact-cost modeling improves robustness under multi-contact stress.
- Evidence claim: the local benchmark shows `0.107 +/- 0.010` success gain over `risk_aware_contact_planner`, `7/7` seed wins, lower budget violations and falls, higher balance/recovery, and decisive ablations.
- Scope claim: strong local evidence, not final ICLR-main readiness.
